Who I Am

Academic Background

My academic journey has always been focused on language as an expression of identity, culture, and creativity — and it was this passion that led me to teach Portuguese as an additional language.

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Languages – Universidade Estácio de Sá (UNESA)
  • Specialist in Literature Teaching – Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Norte (UFRN)
  • Specialist in Applied Linguistics for Language Teaching – Universidade Federal de Mato Grosso do Sul (UFMS)
  • Specialist in Teaching Portuguese for Foreigners – Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio)
  • Master’s Student in Teaching Portuguese as a Foreign Language – Faculdade de Ciências Sociais e Humanas, Universidade NOVA de Lisboa

Hello! I’m Simone, the creator of Zuca Tuga — a welcoming and creative space dedicated to teaching Portuguese to foreigners. I’m Brazilian, I live in Portugal, and I deeply believe in the power of languages to bring people, cultures, and stories closer together. Over time, I’ve built a solid career as a teacher, working with students from different nationalities, profiles, and backgrounds. I believe that learning a new language goes far beyond grammar: it’s an encounter with other ways of living, thinking, and feeling. And that’s what drives me as an educator. I value each class as a genuine exchange opportunity. These are unique moments, full of listening, discovery, and warmth — moments when I not only teach, but also learn from each student. This interaction, to me, is what gives meaning to my work and fuels my desire to continue on this path.

You’re very welcome to our Zuca Tuga community!
